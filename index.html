Dr. Jose Rizal

National Hero of the Philippines

Here's a time line of Dr. Rizal's life:

  • 1861 - Born in Calamba, Laguna, Philippines
  • 1872 - Enrolls at the Ateneo Municipal de Manila, where he studies for six years
  • 1878 - Completes his Bachelor of Arts at the University of Santo Tomas
  • 1882 - Pursues further studies in Spain, taking up medicine and liberal arts
  • 1887 - Writes his first novel, "Noli Me Tangere," which exposes the social issues and injustices in the Philippines under Spanish colonial rule
  • 1888 - Completes his medical degree at the Universidad Central de Madrid
  • 1889 - Publishes his second novel, "El Filibusterismo," a continuation of the themes explored in his first work
  • 1892 - Returns to the Philippines and establishes the La Liga Filipina, an organization advocating for reforms
  • 1896 - Arrested and exiled to Dapitan, Mindanao by Spanish authorities
  • 1898 - The Philippine Revolution against Spanish rule begins
  • 1898 - The United States defeats Spain in the Spanish-American War, acquiring the Philippines in the process
